# Shipping

Use the **Shipping** settings to define delivery options for your store. Each entry represents one shipping method, like ‚ÄúStandard Shipping‚Äù or ‚ÄúFree Shipping‚Äù.

## ‚ûï Add a Shipping Method

1. Go to the **Shipping** collection under **Settings**.
2. Click **"Create New"**.

### Fill in the Fields:

#### **Shipping Name**

- The name of this shipping option, e.g. `Standard Shipping`, `Express Delivery`.

#### **Enabled**

- Toggle this on to make the shipping method available at checkout.

#### **Location**

- Select a location where this shipping method should apply. This connects to your **Locations** collection.

## üì¶ Add a Shipping Provider

Inside the **Shipping Provider** section, click **‚ÄúAdd Block‚Äù** and select:

### `Custom Shipping`

This is a simple flat-rate shipping option with optional features.

#### **Label Shown to Customer**

- The label customers will see during checkout.
- Example: `Standard Shipping`, `2-Day Express`.

#### **Base Rate (flat rate)**

- The flat cost for this shipping method.
- Example: `5` (for $5.00 shipping).

#### **Free Shipping Over (optional)**

- Leave blank to always charge the base rate.
- Set a number to offer free shipping for orders over that amount.

#### **Estimated Delivery (optional)**

- Example: `3-5 business days`.

#### **Notes (optional)**

- Any extra message shown to the customer. Example: ‚ÄúShips from our warehouse in 24 hours.‚Äù
